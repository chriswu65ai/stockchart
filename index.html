Share Price Timeline

Upload an Excel file containing at least two columns: Date and Price. Optional columns: Event and Note for annotated bubbles.

Upload your file to render the chart.

How to use

  • Scroll to zoom horizontally, or pinch on touch devices.
  • Drag the chart to pan through time, or use the timeline scroll bar.
  • Hover event/note bubbles to view annotation text, and click a bubble when it contains a URL.
