Upload your file to render the chart.

How to use

  • Upload an Excel file containing at least two columns: Date and Price. Optional columns: Event and Comment for annotated bubbles.
  • Scroll to zoom horizontally, or pinch on touch devices.
  • Drag the chart to pan through time, or use the timeline window bar (drag center to move, drag edges to expand/compress timeframe).
  • Hover event/comment bubbles to view annotation text, and click a bubble when it contains a URL.
